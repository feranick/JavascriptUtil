The program allows the choice of calculating the profit on the wholesale price or on the the retail price.

  1. Profit on wholesale: the profit is calculated on the wholesale price. This profit is calculated starting from the price input by the user (it can be either in the "Wholesale", in the final "Retail", or in the "Wholesale + Tax" field).
  2. Profit on retail: the profit is calculated on the retail price. This margin is calculated starting from the price input by the user (it can be either in the "Wholesale", in the final "Retail", or in the "Wholesale + Tax" field).

By selecting the proper tax percentage, the program calculates the tax amounts for: the wholesale price ("Tax credit"), the profit ("Tax profit") and the total final price ("Tax"). A discount can be also be applied. The program calculates the discounted retail price ("Disc. price"), the margin of profit on the discounted price ("Disc. profit") and the total tax amount for the discounted price ("Tax disc. price").
